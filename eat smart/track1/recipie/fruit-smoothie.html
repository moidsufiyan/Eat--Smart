Fruit Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Almond milk
  • Honey

Instructions

  • Blend banana, strawberries, blueberries, almond milk, and honey in a blender.
  • Serve chilled and enjoy a refreshing smoothie!
